Registered Leadership

Our Executive Committee.

Valmartin Healthcare Association is led by an executive committee of six, formally registered with the Senior Divisional Officer for Fako under reference No. 268/G.37/A3/SAAJP.

AW
President, Founder

Dr. Azua Ekokobe Wilfred

Medical Doctor, MPH (Community Health), PGCert Public Health (University of Manchester). Atlantic Fellow for Health Equity. Founder of Valmartin Healthcare. Regional health programme leader with over seven years of experience managing health, nutrition, and emergency programmes across Cameroon's North West and South West regions.

EA
Vice President

Mr. Ekokobe Aloysius

Registered Nurse. Deputy to the President with operational oversight of clinical services and community engagement.

NL
Secretary General

Dr. Ngashu Louis Tiku

Medical Doctor. Co-leads clinical service delivery and serves as the institutional secretary for Valmartin Healthcare Association.

LP
Financial Secretary

Ms. Lekeaka Prisca Ndohbegang M.

Pharmacist. Leads the pro-pharmacy and oversees procurement, stock management, and pharmaceutical financial records.

JN
Treasurer

Ms. Jennifer Ngeso

Accountant. Manages the financial operations of the association, including budgeting, reporting, and donor accountability.

CE
Adviser

Ms. Celine Enow Eiwuchukwu

Registered Nurse. Senior advisor to the executive committee with longstanding clinical and community health experience.
