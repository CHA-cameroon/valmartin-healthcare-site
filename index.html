Founded 2021
Our story

Born from a belief: every life deserves care.

Valmartin Healthcare opened its doors in 2021 with a simple conviction: that quality healthcare should not depend on where you live or what you can afford. From a single building in Saker, Limbe, we set out to be a clinic the community could trust at any hour.

In April 2024, Valmartin was formally registered as a non-profit healthcare association under the laws of Cameroon. That registration codified what our patients already knew: we are here to serve, not to extract.

01

Facilitate access to health facilities through emergency response, ambulance referrals, and 24-hour clinical care.

02

Advance research on diseases and outbreaks through community sensitisation, surveillance, and reporting.

03

Investigate and dismantle the barriers that prevent communities from accessing quality healthcare.
