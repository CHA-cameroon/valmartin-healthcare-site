Home/Programmes

Beyond the clinic walls, into the community.

Valmartin's programmes take healthcare directly to the people who need it most, free outreaches, mother and baby celebrations, and ongoing health education.

Annual, Free

Mama and Baby Day

A flagship celebration of every mother who has trusted us with her care. Free entry, free gifts, and expert sessions on antenatal care, safe delivery, breastfeeding, newborn care, fertility, and family planning.

Open to all ANC mothers, breastfeeding mamas, and their partners. Past editions have included expert talks, breastfeeding clinics with hands-on guidance, mama and baby gift packs, and fertility and cycle calculation sessions.

Register for the next event
Recurring, Free and Subsidised

Medical Outreach Campaigns

Multi-day medical outreaches delivered in Saker and surrounding communities. Past editions have offered free consultations, free health education, free family planning, and free HIV screening, alongside heavily subsidised malaria, blood sugar, hepatitis B, blood group, and echography services.

A medical doctor remains available 24/7 throughout outreach periods to attend to patients with urgent health needs.

Ongoing, Free

Community Health Education

Health does not begin in the consultation room. Through clinic-based talks, community sessions, and our partnership networks, we deliver continuous health education on disease prevention, maternal nutrition, family planning, danger signs in pregnancy, and newborn care.

Where it helps, we deliver these sessions in Pidgin English alongside English and French to make sure no one is left out by language.

Internal, Annual

Staff Recognition Programme

Our people are our most important asset. The Valmartin Recognition Programme honours staff and partners who have gone the extra mile in service of our patients and our community, with formal certificates of recognition awarded throughout the year.

Want to support a programme? We welcome partners.

Our programmes are made possible by community goodwill and the support of partners. If you are an NGO, donor, faith institution, or corporate partner, we would love to talk.
