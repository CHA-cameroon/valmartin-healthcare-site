Home/Services

Care that meets you
where you are.

Ten core services delivered by a multidisciplinary team, available 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

01

General Consultation

Walk-in or scheduled consultations with our medical team. Whether you need a routine check-up, are managing a chronic condition, or have an urgent concern, a doctor is on duty 24 hours a day to see you.

  • Adult and paediatric consultations
  • Chronic disease management (hypertension, diabetes)
  • Acute illness diagnosis and treatment
  • Preventive health check-ups
02

Maternity and Safe Delivery

From the first antenatal visit to the safe delivery of your baby and beyond. Our maternity service is built around dignity, safety, and support, with regular Mama and Baby Days dedicated to new mothers.

  • Antenatal care (ANC) and ANC discounts
  • Safe delivery, day or night
  • Postnatal care for mothers and newborns
  • Breastfeeding support and counselling
  • Family planning consultations
  • Childhood vaccinations
03

Laboratory and Diagnostics

An on-site laboratory delivering accurate, timely results so your treatment plan can begin without delay.

  • Malaria testing
  • Blood sugar testing
  • Hepatitis B screening
  • Blood group typing
  • HIV screening (free during outreach campaigns)
  • Echography (ultrasound)
  • General haematology and biochemistry
04

Pro-Pharmacy

Our on-site pro-pharmacy means you do not have to leave the clinic to fill your prescription. We stock a wide range of essential medications and partner with reputable suppliers to keep our shelves reliable.

  • Prescription fulfilment from your Valmartin consultation
  • Over-the-counter medications
  • Counselling on safe medication use
  • Affordable pricing on essential drugs
05

Hospitalisation and Minor Surgery

For patients who need close monitoring or minor procedures, we offer inpatient beds and a clean, well-equipped procedure space staffed by our experienced clinical team.

  • Short-stay hospitalisation
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Wound care and dressing
  • Intravenous therapy and monitoring
06

Home Visits and Community Outreach

Some patients cannot come to us, so we go to them. Our home visit service, combined with regular community outreaches, brings clinical care directly into homes and neighbourhoods.

  • Home consultations for elderly and immobile patients
  • Wound care and follow-up visits at home
  • Community outreach campaigns with free consultations
  • Free health education in Pidgin and English
  • Mama and Baby Days for new mothers
07

Emergency Response and Ambulance Referrals

As part of our official mandate, Valmartin facilitates emergency response and ambulance referrals to the nearest higher-level health facility when a case exceeds our scope.

  • Initial emergency stabilisation
  • Coordinated referral to district or regional hospitals
  • 24-hour availability
08

Health Education and Counselling

Good health is built on good information. Our team offers individual and group health education, both at the clinic and through community sessions.

  • Pre-test and post-test HIV counselling
  • Reproductive health and family planning counselling
  • Nutrition education for mothers and children
  • Disease prevention talks during outreaches

Ready to book? We're ready to see you.

Same-day appointments are usually available. For pricing on specific services, please contact us directly.
